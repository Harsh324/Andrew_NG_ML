** Machine Learning had grown up as a sub-field of AI or 
    artificial intelligence

** Usecases for it may be speech recognition, computer vision
    for Google Maps, Street View images and advertising, 
    AI for augmented reality to combating payment fraud, 
    self-driving car team, factory, large-scale agriculture,
    health care, e-commerce etc.

** There is a term Artificial General Intelligence or AGI.
    This means building machines as intelligent as humans.


**  According to a study by McKinsey, AI and machine learning
    is estimated to create an additional 13 trillion US dollars
    of value annually by the year 2030.
